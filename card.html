Cat

All kittens are born with blue eyes.

  • #1: Cats, not dogs, are the most common pets in America.
  • #2: A cat can be either right-pawed or left-pawed.
  • #3: A cat can jump as much as 7 times its height.
  • #4: A cat sees about 6 times better than a human at night.

Unlike dogs, cats do not have a sweet tooth. Scientists believe this is due to a mutation in a key taste receptor. When a cat chases its prey, it keeps its head level. Dogs and humans bob their heads up and down. The technical term for a cat’s hairball is a “bezoar”. A group of cats is called a “clowder". A cat can’t climb head first down a tree because every claw on a cat’s paw points the same way. To get down from a tree, a cat must back down. Cats make about 100 different sounds. Dogs make only about 10.
